About Hurricane Console

Parses the National Hurricane Center's Tropical Weather Discussion — the forecasters' own written reasoning — into map features, entirely in your browser. Solid shapes come from explicit coordinates in the text; dashed ◇ INFERRED shapes are best-guess positions read from prose, and are drawn differently on purpose.

Unofficial — not for life-safety decisions. This is a visualization aid built on a heuristic parser; it can misread or miss features. Always follow the official products at hurricanes.gov and the guidance of your local emergency management.
